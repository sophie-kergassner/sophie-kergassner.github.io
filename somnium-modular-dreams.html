Somnium – Modular Dreams

We are dreaming. Dreams are a way to our subconscious, they are unsteady. Somnium ‐ Modular Dreams presents an interactive media installation that displays our dream visually, audibly, with atmosphere, and with light. It was created withing 5 months at the Stuttgart Media University with a team of 14 students. It was honored at the 2021 Talent Award of ADC, and was finalist of the CommAwards 2021.
